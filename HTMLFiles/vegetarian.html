    Top Vegetarian/Vegan Restaurants in SF

    Click on the Restaurant logo to go to their website!

    Wildseed has plant-based meals with locally sourced fresh produce from the Bay Area. Many of its meals are gluten free, with vegan options. It's dishes include items like Chicory Salad, Pea Tendrils, and Curried Caulifolower for those with allium allergies.

    Golden Era is a vegan restaurant with cusines of Vietnamese, Thai, Chinese, and Indian. It won the top Vegetarian/Vegan Restaurant in the Bay Area 2021 in the San Francisco Chronicles!

    Shizen offers a diverse choice of sushi, with most of them vegan and gluten free! Their food is plant based to reduce the amount of harm done in the ocean and conserve some fish. The reviews are ravishing!

    Beit Rima offers authentic Middle Eastern food! Beit Rima has vegetarian dishes as well. It was featured in top 17 Bay Area restaurants in the Bay Area and top 100 restaurants in the San Francsico Chronicles!

    Nourish Cafe has food that is 100% plant based, using non-GMO ingredients and organic, whole foods. Their ingredients are clean and sustainable, and even their takeout containers are compostable! For healthy, organic, clean food, check out Nourish Cafe SF!

    Baia offers Italian inspired plant based comfort food! Their menu items are rotated seasonally, with them reinventing certain Italian dishes. The pasta is fresh, and gluten free options are available on pizza and pastas.

    If you're looking for scrumptious, plant-based, Mexican, food, try Gracias Madre! Their ingredients are sustainable and committed to healthy and organic food.

    Cha-Ya serves vegan Japanese food!
