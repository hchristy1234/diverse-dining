    About Us

    Do you have an allergy or diet that limits your restaurant choices? We have a solution for you. Diverse Dining is an app that shows you restaurants that are friendly to your food restrictions. Created by 4 Kode With Klossy Scholars, we were inspired to make this website after seeing the struggles that many of our peers and loved ones go through everyday, trying to find a restaurant that suits their needs. We hope that this website will allow people to find delicious meals, no matter their dietary restrictions.
